What if hospitality businesses could share back-of-house infrastructure while maintaining distinct front-of-house identities?

## The Concept

Multiple hospitality concepts operating from shared:
- Kitchen infrastructure
- Supply chain and purchasing
- Administrative systems
- Staff training and development

While maintaining unique:
- Brand identity
- Guest experience
- Menu and offerings
- Service style

## Status

Early concept phase. Researching models and building the business case.
